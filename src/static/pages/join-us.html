Senior Big Data Engineer

Location: Geneva, Switzerland / San Fransisco, CA
Type: Full Time.
Experience: Expert

About ProtonMail

ProtonMail is a fast growing startup that is building the secure and private online communications platforms of the future. We believe that privacy matters and protecting this right is crucial for protecting freedom. The impact of ensuring privacy and freedom of speech for all will be significant and far reaching, and will change the world forever.

ProtonMail has 10 employees located at offices in Geneva and San Francisco. You will be joining our development team and helping design the multi-petabyte, high availability storage systems required as we scale our capacity.

What You Need for this Position
  • Experience designing, building, and managing large storage systems for web applications.
  • Prior experience working on big data projects.
What You Will Be Doing
  • Lead the design and implementation of a high performance/high reliability storage system to hold petabytes of encrypted email data.
  • Building in multiple levels of software redundancy to provide 99.9999% uptime.
  • Collaborating closely with storage and infrastructure experts at CERN and our hardware partners in Switzerland who are handling the hardware aspects of ProtonMail's storage systems.
  • Researching the latest technical options available so we remain on the cutting edge of storage.
Top Reasons to Work with Us
  1. Competitive salary and benefits, along with stock options
  2. Many career advancement opportunities. As an early employee, you will be able to assume a leadership role as the company grows.
  3. Casual and exciting work environment. We are a fast growing startup with a rapidly increasing user base, international media attention, and a fast paced development cycle. Things are constantly changing as we expand so there is never a dull moment.
  4. Do something meaningful. We are restoring privacy rights online, and have global support for our mission. The internet of tomorrow will be one where all communications are encrypted end-to-end and we are leading this revolution, starting with email.
Where We're Heading

Since May 2014 we have grown from 3 employees to 10 and from 1000 users to 250,000. We are quickly taking off and constantly expanding the team to improve our product. In addition to developing our webmail interface, we are also building out mobile applications. We are also expanding beyond email into secure chat, and secure file storage. Join one of the most dynamic and fast growing startups in Switzerland and SF.
